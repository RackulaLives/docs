Plan your homelab rack layout with Rackula. No account required, runs entirely in your browser.

<Steps>

1. **Open Rackula**

   Head to [count.racku.la](https://count.racku.la) — the app loads instantly.

2. **Create your first rack**

   Click **New Rack** and set your rack height in U (rack units). Standard sizes are 42U, 25U, or 12U.

3. **Add devices**

   Drag devices from the library onto your rack. Resize them to match the U height of your actual gear.

4. **Export your layout**

   Export as PNG, SVG, or PDF for documentation or sharing.

</Steps>
